Who are we?

  • We aim to encourage children who have pediatric cancer by providing various hospice care services launched by volunteers from Korea.

  • We are young and full of passion.

  • We know how COVID19 impacted on the social activities of kids who have pediatric cancer.

  • Therefore, we would like to provide young and joyful social activities for them. By cooperating with Korea Pediatric Cancer Foundation, we commit to come up with many cheerful projects.

Things we do

We believe letters would help the children not only gain dreams but also feel socially connected.

The letters are written by volunteers from middle school to college, and it contains who they are, how they are overcoming their dilemmas, and why they are interested in helping out pediatric cancer children.

The letters also have cute and colorful pictures drawn by volunteers!

Zoom meeting team of volunteers

Some hospice care services provide education, like basic academic studies. In order to raise interest in education, we provide fun teaching activities, such as learning Korean by interacting with K-culture or K-pop! Moreover, we would like to play small games, like bingo and card games, and make children feel socially connected!
